The best way to treat the common cold is to

(A) drink plenty of soda and eat junk food

(B) ** drink plenty of water and get plenty of rest

(C) take over the counter medications that only treat the symptoms

(D) take opioids to ease the discomfort

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Drink plenty of fluids. Water, juice, clear broth or warm lemon water are good choices. Sip warm liquids. Rest. Adjust your room’s temperature and humidity. Soothe a sore throat. Try saline nasal drops or sprays.

Concept note-2: -Good hydration helps clear congestion. When you’re sick, make sure you get eight cups of water a day. A better hydrated body helps the immune system stop the cold symptoms.

Concept note-3: -There is no cure for a cold. To feel better, you should get lots of rest and drink plenty of fluids. Over-the-counter medicines may help ease symptoms but will not make your cold go away any faster. Always read the label and use medications as directed.

Concept note-4: -Hot water with honey and lemon Stayed hydrated is essential to good health, so give your body a helping hand when suffering from a cold and stay hydrated by drinking filtered water.
