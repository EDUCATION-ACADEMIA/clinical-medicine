Vanessa is unaware of the three types of bacteria shapes. She has to research it for a science project. Which article is provides an accurate answer?

(A) IncrediblyCorrectFacts.net-“the three types of bacteria shapes are bacilli, coconut, and spirit”

(B) RealScience.io-“the three types of bacterial stages are bacilli, cocci, and spirilla

(C) ** MicrobiologyForDummies.org-“bacteria can appear rod-shaped (bacilli), spherical (cocci), and spiral-shaped (spirilla)”

(D) papajohns.com-“Better Ingredients. Better Pizza”

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bacteria are classified into five groups according to their basic shapes: spherical (cocci), rod (bacilli), spiral (spirilla), comma (vibrios) or corkscrew (spirochaetes).

Concept note-2: -Individual bacteria can assume one of three basic shapes: spherical (coccus), rodlike (bacillus), or curved (vibrio, spirillum, or spirochete).
