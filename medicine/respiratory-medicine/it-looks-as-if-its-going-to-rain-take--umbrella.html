It looks as if it’s going to rain. Take ____ umbrella!

(A) the

(B) a

(C) ** an

(D) -

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is continuous present tense.

Concept note-2: -If it rains, I will take an umbrella. If it rains, I will take an umbrella-Future tense. Dependent clause is used when we say ‘If’. The future condition describes something that could happen with a condition in the future. Examples: If I go to the beach tomorrow, I will bring my kids.

Concept note-3: -The article ‘An’ is used before those words which start with a vowel sound. ‘Umbrella’ is a vowel sound. Thus, the sentence would become-What is this ? It’s an umbrella.
