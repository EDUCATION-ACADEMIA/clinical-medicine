Defects in embryogenesis is the main etiology of developing congenital heart disease. Which trimester is the most important period during the pregnancy?

(A) ** 1st trimester

(B) 2nd trimester.

(C) 3rd trimester.

(D) all trimesters have the same importance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first trimester is the most crucial to your baby’s development. During this period, your baby’s body structure and organ systems develop. Most miscarriages and birth defects occur during this period. Your body also undergoes major changes during the first trimester.

Concept note-2: -Birth defects can happen at any time during pregnancy. But most happen during the first 3 months of pregnancy (also called first trimester), when your baby’s organs are forming. Birth defects also can happen later in pregnancy, when your baby’s organs are still growing and developing.
